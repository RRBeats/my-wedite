Planets

Our solar system is home to eight unique planets, each with its own characteristics. From the scorching surface of Mercury to the icy expanses of Neptune, there's a lot to explore.

Stars

Stars are massive spheres of burning gas that light up our night sky. The most famous star, our Sun, is essential for life on Earth. There are many types of stars, including red giants and white dwarfs.

Galaxies

Galaxies are vast collections of stars, planets, and other cosmic materials. Our Milky Way galaxy is just one of billions in the universe. Some galaxies are spiral-shaped, like our own, while others are elliptical or irregular.
